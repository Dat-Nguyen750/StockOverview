About Stock Evaluator Pro

AI-powered investment analysis for the modern investor

Our Mission

To democratize access to professional-grade stock analysis by combining advanced AI technology with comprehensive financial data.

Stock Evaluator Pro was built to help individual investors make more informed decisions by providing the same level of analysis that institutional investors have access to, but in an easy-to-use, affordable platform.

Technology Stack
Backend
  • FastAPI - High-performance web framework
  • Flask - Web application framework
  • Python - Core programming language
  • Docker - Containerization
AI & Data
  • Google Gemini - Advanced AI analysis
  • Financial Modeling Prep - Financial data
  • SERP API - News sentiment analysis
  • Pandas - Data processing
Scoring System

Our comprehensive scoring system evaluates stocks across five key dimensions:

Financial Health (35%)

Revenue growth, profitability, cash flow, debt management, and financial stability metrics.

Business Fundamentals (20%)

Revenue model, competitive moat, industry position, and business model sustainability.

Valuation (20%)

P/E ratios, PEG, Price/FCF, DCF analysis, and relative valuation metrics.

Growth Potential (15%)

Analyst forecasts, R&D investment, TAM analysis, and growth trajectory indicators.

Sentiment & Red Flags (10%)

News sentiment, insider trading patterns, ESG factors, and potential risk indicators.
